Particulate Matter (PM2.5)

Tiny particles that enter deep into lungs and bloodstream
  • Common sources: Dust, smoke, vehicle exhaust
  • Health risks: Breathing problems, heart issues
  • WHO guideline: 15 µg/m³ annual mean
Good Moderate Poor
Current: 45 µg/m³

Tip: Use HEPA air purifiers & keep windows open when outdoor air is clean.

Carbon Dioxide (CO₂)

Colorless, odorless gas from breathing, combustion & poor ventilation
  • Common sources: People, gas stoves, crowded rooms
  • Health risks: Drowsiness, headaches, reduced focus
  • Good indoor level: < 800 ppm
Good Moderate Poor
Current: 620 ppm

Tip: Increase fresh air supply & use CO₂ monitors in crowded spaces.

Volatile Organic Compounds (VOC)

Gases released from paints, cleaners, furniture & new materials
  • Common sources: Paints, adhesives, air fresheners, carpets
  • Health risks: Eye/nose/throat irritation, headaches
  • Good indoor level: < 0.5 mg/m³
Good Moderate Poor
Current: 0.38 mg/m³

Tip: Choose low-VOC products & ventilate well after painting or cleaning.

Gas or Ozone

Strong-smelling gas (ozone) or other indoor pollutants from devices & reactions
  • Common sources: Printers, air purifiers, outdoor smog, cleaners
  • Health risks: Lung irritation, worsened asthma
  • WHO guideline: < 100 µg/m³ (8-hour mean)
Good Moderate Poor
Current: 28 µg/m³

Tip: Avoid ozone-generating devices indoors & ensure good ventilation.

Temperature

Optimal indoor temperature affects comfort, productivity & health
  • Ideal range: 20–26 °C for most indoor environments
  • Too cold: Increased risk of respiratory infections
  • Too hot: Fatigue, dehydration, reduced concentration
Cold Comfortable Hot
Current: 24.8 °C

Tip: Maintain 20–24 °C for optimal comfort and energy efficiency.

Humidity

Relative humidity affects mold growth, dust mites & respiratory comfort
  • Ideal range: 40–60% relative humidity (RH)
  • High humidity: Mold growth, dust mite proliferation
  • Low humidity: Dry skin, irritated airways, static electricity
Dry Comfortable Humid
Current: 58% RH

Tip: Use dehumidifiers in humid conditions & humidifiers in dry winter air.
